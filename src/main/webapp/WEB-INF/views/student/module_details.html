Description

This comprehensive module explores the nature of stress and provides practical strategies for managing it effectively. You'll learn to identify your personal stress triggers, understand the physiological and psychological effects of stress, and develop a toolkit of evidence-based coping mechanisms.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the biological and psychological mechanisms of stress
  • Identify personal stress triggers and patterns
  • Learn and practice effective coping strategies
  • Develop a personalized stress management plan
  • Recognize when to seek professional help

Prerequisites

No prior knowledge required. This module is suitable for anyone interested in learning stress management techniques.
